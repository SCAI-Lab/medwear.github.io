About the MEDWEAR Project

MEDWEAR is a research initiative funded by the ETH Domain Open Research Data (ORD) programme. It aims to develop an open, community-driven standard for raw wearable sensor data to improve interoperability, reusability, and integration across clinical, research, and industrial domains.

Timeline

  • Project Duration: January 1, 2025 – May 31, 2025
  • Principal Investigator: Diego Paez-Granados, ETH Zurich

Funding & Institutional Support

MEDWEAR is supported under the ORD Measure 1 (Contribute Track) and hosted by the SCAI Lab at ETH Zurich.
