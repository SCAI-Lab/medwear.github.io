Welcome to MEDWEAR

MEDWEAR is a collaborative project funded by ETH Zurich’s Open Research Data initiative. We aim to define open standards for raw wearable sensor data that are FAIR, interoperable, and vendor-agnostic.

Through community-driven development, we are creating:

  • Open schemas based on Open mHealth
  • ROS 2 message definitions for robotics and healthcare
  • Conversion libraries and educational materials
